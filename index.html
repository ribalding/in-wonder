What's your ideal vacation destination?

Your ideal destination is Ibiza

Your relaxed and carefree nature lends itself well to the beautiful beaches of this
Mediterrranean Spanish island. While you can spend your days
relaxing and taking in the sun, the nightlife offers more exciting opportunities.
The island is well known for it's nightlife and electronic music scene.

Your ideal destination is New Zealand

Having been the filming location for the "Lord of the Rings" trilogy,
it should come as no surprise that New Zealand is the home of truly breathtaking scenery.
One of the last islands to every be inahbited by humans, nearly all of the natural beauty
remains intact and is a constant draw for nature enthusiasts of all stripes.

Your ideal destination is Iceland

The stark and quiet beauty of this sparsely populated European island nation will be perfect for you.
While nightlife and other social activies are minimal, the mountains, glaciers, northern lights,
lava fields and black sand beaches will captivate the more reflective, introspctive type
and all appreciators of beauty.

Your ideal destination is Amsterdam

History lovers will flock to the famous capital of the Netherlands.
This canal based city is not only historic (having been home to Anne Frank,
Rembrandt and Spinoza, as well as the world's oldest stock exchange),
but beautiful as well. It's famously liberal policies regarding recreational drug use
has made it a hot spot for young tourists for many years.
